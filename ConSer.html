Multiexperience

Multiexperience involves various permutations of modalities, such as touch and gesture.
Devices and apps that people use which involve such touchpoints are examples of multiexperience.
Multiexperience involves the creation of applications that are porposefully built based on various touchpoint-specific adaptations.
MXDPs are app development platforms that are used to develop chat, voice, augmented reality, and wearable experiences, as well as mobile and web apps.

Distributed Cloud

Distributed Cloud Computing generalises the cloud computing model to position, process, and serve data and applications from geographically distributed sites to meet requirements for performance, redundancy and regulations.
Using distributed cloud computing gives users the ability to not maintain and operate their own IT infrastructure, and shift CAPEX to OPEX by using the utility-like model of purchasing computing and storage on demand.
